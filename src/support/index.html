Sheet Selector Excel AddIn Support Page

Usage

Open the Taskpane

Click the button on the Ribbon to open the addin taskpane.

Filter Worksheets

The taskpane shows all worksheets in your workbook.
When you input a word into the text box, worksheets which includes the word in their name will appears on the list. The filter ignors case.

Activate a Sheet

Once you select a name of worksheet on the taskpane, the worksheet will be activated.
